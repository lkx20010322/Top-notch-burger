Our Story

Top Notch Burgers is a burger business that operates in Melbourne, Australia. Top Notch Burgers was started in 2018 by siblings Sol and Jillian Bartlet. After working in the industry for many years they saw a need for a fresh take on the burger business. Basing their new menu on fresh American and South American flavours, they offer a range of tasty and fashionable quick meals. With vegan and vegetarian options, healthy options are a big focus for this business. The Top Notch Burgers kitchen is proud to be located in the inner North. Drop in anytime and talk to us about our catering options.
